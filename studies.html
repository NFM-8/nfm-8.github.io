NFM-8

This is the list of all the courses I have officially completed at the Tampere university of technology. That's 290 ECTS total, if I computed correctly. Okay, I have also done a Bachelor's thesis (8 ECTS) and a Master's one (30 ECTS) but I don't count them.

I'm a living counterexample for statements like "education makes people competent" or "formal education is always good." I'm not nearly creative enough for any real research work and far away from productive enough for any real, productive work. Despite all that formal education. Money well spent, someone might say.

The observation described above is not meant as a lament. It just summarizes conclusions I have made, based on my observations. The "truth", as I have perceived it. So please, don't take it too seriously or negatively.
